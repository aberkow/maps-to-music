Maps to Music

Hear where you're going.

This is a sonified map of the world. In short, sonification is the process of turning data into sound. Sonar is a good example. If you type in any two cities you can drive to and press play. You'll hear the directions "performed" by a pair of synthesizers. They get their instructions from the latitude, longitude, and time of every change in direction.

Developed by Adam J. Berkowitz with LeafletJS and ToneJS.
